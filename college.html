Program Overview

Our College Robotics program bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world applications, providing hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies that prepare students for careers in automation, AI, and embedded systems.

Microcontroller Projects

Hands-on experience with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and other popular microcontroller platforms.

IoT & Home Automation

C & Python Programming

AI & ML Basics

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning concepts with practical applications.
