School Robotics Program

Our school program is designed to introduce young students to the exciting world of robotics through hands-on, age-appropriate projects that make learning fun and engaging.

Program Highlights

  • Basic robotics concepts for ages 6-13
  • LEGO Mindstorms and other child-friendly platforms
  • Project-based learning approach
  • Creative problem-solving activities

Learning Outcomes

  • Develop logical thinking and creativity
  • Introduction to basic programming concepts
  • Teamwork and collaboration skills
  • Confidence in STEM subjects
